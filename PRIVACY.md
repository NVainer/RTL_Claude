# Privacy Policy — Claude RTL

_Last updated: 2026-06-28_

Claude RTL is a browser extension that adjusts text direction (right-to-left) for
Hebrew and Arabic on claude.ai. Your privacy is simple to describe:

- **No data collection.** The extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any
  personal or usage data.
- **No external servers.** It makes no network requests. Everything runs locally
  in your browser.
- **Local storage only.** The single piece of state it stores is an on/off
  preference, saved with `chrome.storage.local` on your own device.
- **No reading of your conversations.** The extension only adjusts the text
  *direction* of page elements; it does not read, store, or send the content of
  your chats.
- **Permissions.** It requests `storage` (to remember the on/off toggle) and host
  access to `claude.ai` / `claude.site` (so the styling can be applied there, and
  nowhere else).
